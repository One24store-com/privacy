  2. Sensitive Information policy

    Accessing background location is crucial for our application, as it involves tracking the location of delivery partners or drivers. In order to provide real-time updates to customers about the status and location of their package, it is necessary to have continuous access to the delivery partner's location, even when the app is running in the background or the screen is locked. When a customer places an order, the delivery app assigns a delivery partner to fulfill the order. The delivery partner then uses the app to navigate to the pickup location and subsequently to the customer's address. In order to provide accurate real-time tracking information to the customer, the app needs to continuously monitor the delivery partner's location, even if the app is not actively being used or if the screen is locked. This is where background location access becomes crucial. By accessing the delivery partner's location in the background, the app can provide accurate updates to the customer about the estimated delivery time, the location of the delivery partner, and any unexpected delays or changes to the delivery route. This level of transparency helps to build trust between the customer and the delivery service, and can ultimately lead to increased customer satisfaction and loyalty. We access background location with the user's consent and with proper consideration for their privacy and security. we adhere to industry standards and best practices for handling sensitive user information, and clearly communicate to users how their location data will be used and protected..
